Daloop Midday Pulse – March 18, 2026

March 18, 2026

MIDDAY PULSE

  • U.S. equities are softer mid-session after an inflation surprise on the wholesale side: SPY 666.31 (-0.67%), QQQ 599.74 (-0.59%), DIA 466.33 (-0.97%), and IWM 248.05 (-0.80%).
  • Risk appetite is cautious and choppy ahead of the Fed decision later today, with market sentiment reading as uncertain.
  • Crypto is under pressure: BTC 71,157 (-3.76%) and ETH 2,173 (-6.24%), with crypto-linked products lagging.

WHAT'S MOVING NOW

  • SWMR 57.90 (+86.77%): outsized debut move after its recent IPO pricing; extremely high volatility is drawing momentum flows.
  • PERF 1.67 (+23.42%): spiking on a reported take-private proposal, pushing the microcap tape higher.
  • NFE 0.91 (-21.30%): heavy selling on restructuring uncertainty, and it is also among today’s most actively traded tickers.
  • M 17.64 (+4.23%): moving higher after earnings as investors weigh better-than-expected quarterly results against a more cautious outlook.

SECTOR HEAT MAP

  • Green: Energy (XLE +0.32%) and Industrials (XLI +0.01%) are holding up best.
  • Red: Health Care (XLV -1.10%), Real Estate (XLRE -0.81%), and Financials (XLF -0.69%) are lagging, with Tech (XLK -0.29%) modestly lower.
  • Broadly, this is a defensive-leaning tape: cyclicals are mixed while rate-sensitive groups are softer.

DEVELOPING STORIES

  • Inflation watch: February PPI ran hot (+0.7% m/m; core +0.5% m/m), keeping the market focused on the Fed’s tone and updated projections this afternoon.
  • Fed risk window: the policy statement, projections, and Powell presser are the main catalysts into the close; expect volatility clusters around the decision.
  • Crypto sensitivity: with BTC and ETH down sharply, watch for spillover to high-beta growth and crypto-linked equities if selling accelerates.

ALERT

  • Watch for fast reversals into the Fed decision. With indexes down but not breaking hard, positioning into the event can exaggerate moves in both directions (especially in high-beta tech and crypto-linked products).
